Detailed Description

SystemController class used to manage power control and various clock system resources on the MSP432P401R MCU.

Member Enumeration Documentation

◆ Clock

enum sjsu::msp432p401r::SystemController::Clock : uint8_t
strong

The available system clocks used to drive various peripheral modules where kAuxiliary, kMaster, kSubsystemMaster, kLowSpeedSubsystemMaster, and kBackup are the primary clock signals.

See also
Figure 6-1 in the MSP432P4xx Reference Manual. https://www.ti.com/lit/ug/slau356i/slau356i.pdf#page=380
Enumerator
kAuxiliary 

Auxiliary clock (ACLK) with a max frequency of 128 kHz.

kMaster 

Master clock (MCLK) that drives the CPU.

kSubsystemMaster 

Subsystem master clock (HSMCLK).

kLowSpeedSubsystemMaster 

Low-speed subsystem master clock (SMCLK).

kBackup 

Low speed backup domain clock (BCLK). The with maximum restricted frequency of 32.768 kHz.

kVeryLowFrequency 

Very low frequency low power clock (LFXTCLK). This clock can be driven by the LFXT oscillator or an external oscillator with a frequency of 32.768 kHz or less in bypass mode.

kLowFrequencyReference 

Low frequency clock (REFOCLK).

kLowFrequency 

Low frequency low power clock (HFXT).

kModule 

Low power module clock (MODCLK).

kSystem 

System oscillator clock (SYSCLK).

◆ Oscillator

enum sjsu::msp432p401r::SystemController::Oscillator : uint8_t
strong

The available internal oscillators for the clock system module.

Enumerator
kLowFrequency 

Low frequency oscillator (LFXT) with frequency of 32.768 kHz.

kVeryLowFrequency 

Ultra low power oscillator (VLO) with typical frequency of 9.4 kHz.

kReference 

Low frequency reference oscillator (REFO) that can be configured to output 32.768 kHz or 128 kHz.

kDigitallyControlled 

Digitally controlled oscillator (DCO) that can be configured to generate a frequency between 1 MHZ and 48 MHz.

kModule 

Low power oscillator with a typical frequency of 25 MHz.

kHighFrequency 

High frequency oscillator (HFXT) which can be driven by an external oscillator or external square wave with frequency ranging from 1 MHz to 48 Mhz.
